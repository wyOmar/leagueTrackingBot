# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FeedWatch ("we", "our", or "the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our services within Discord.

## 1. Data We Collect
When you interact with the Bot, we collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our tracking services:
* **Discord Data:** Your Discord User ID, Guild (Server) IDs where you use the Bot, and your server-specific opt-in preferences.
* **Riot Games Data:** When you link an account, we securely store your Riot PUUID, Game Name, Tagline, region, current rank, and recent match history statistics.

*Note: We do not have access to, nor do we store, your Riot Games password, email, or any sensitive login credentials.*

## 2. How We Use Your Data
The data we collect is used strictly for the functionality of the Bot:
* To fetch live game statuses and post-match statistics from the Riot Games API.
* To display your account information and match results in the servers where you have explicitly opted in.
* To manage your per-server visibility toggles and preferences.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
All user data is stored securely in an encrypted cloud database (MongoDB Atlas). We implement industry-standard access controls to prevent unauthorized access to your data. 

## 4. Data Sharing
We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Your data is only shared with:
* **Discord:** To display your match results via chat embeds.
* **Riot Games:** We send your encrypted PUUID/Riot ID to the official Riot Games API to fetch your match history.

## 5. Data Retention & Your Rights
Your data is retained for as long as you have a Riot account linked to the Bot. You have the right to control and delete your data at any time:
* **Immediate Deletion:** You can permanently delete a linked Riot account from our database by running the `/account remove` command in Discord.
* **Opting Out:** You can hide your tracking data in specific servers without deleting your global profile by using the `/accounts` dashboard.
* **Manual Erasure:** If you no longer have access to Discord or wish to request a full wipe of your data, you can contact us in our support server.

## 6. Policy Updates
We may update this Privacy Policy as we add new features or as API requirements change. If significant changes are made, we will notify users via the Bot's announcement channels or support server.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have questions regarding your privacy or wish to submit a data deletion request, please reach out to us in our official Support Server: https://discord.gg/ghyTQPPqEP
